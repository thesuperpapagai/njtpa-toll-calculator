Tolling Info

New Jersey Turnpike

The New Jersey Turnpike uses a Closed Tolled System. This means that when you enter the Highway, you recieve a ticket and you are charged for the exact miles you traveled when you leave the turnpike.

Garden State Parkway

The Garden State Parkway uses an Open Tolled System. This means that flat tolls are collected at toll booths located on the freeway and off select exit ramps.
